Free vs Full

Free stays capable while Full removes limits for heavier setups.

Feature Free Full
Shortcut mapping (knobs, faders, buttons) Yes Yes
Macro mapping (knobs, faders, buttons) Yes Yes
App or system volume mapping Yes Yes
Per-device presets and quick switching Yes Yes
App targeting Yes Yes
LED feedback Yes Yes
MIDI Thru (requires a virtual MIDI port) Yes Yes
X-Touch One motorized fader and LCD feedback Yes Yes
Simple audio sample playback No Yes
Active devices at a time 1 Multiple
Presets per device Up to 2 Unlimited
Mappings per preset Up to 6 Unlimited

Full unlocks

  • Unlimited presets per device
  • Unlimited mappings per preset
  • Multiple active devices at once
